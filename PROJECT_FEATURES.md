"# Implemented Features"   
##T-10: Discount & Reward System
Features:

The system tracks the number of rides completed between the same companions.

After reaching specific milestones, users receive progressive discounts on future rides.

Dynamic discount tiers (e.g. 5 rides → 5%, 10 rides → 10%).

Rewards can be redeemed as ride credits or discount vouchers.

Encourages loyalty and long-term ride companionship.
